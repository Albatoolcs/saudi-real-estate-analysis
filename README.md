# saudi-real-estate-analysis
Data cleaning and analysis for saudi real estate market using pandas
#Project Overview
A data-driven analysis designed to answer a crucial investment question for a client: "Is investing in the Saudi real estate market financially viable, and where are the best opportunities?"

To provide an accurate and strategic answer, I analyzed total real estate sales across all quarters of 2025, evaluating total sales volume per city against the number of available assets and auction trends.
#Definition of Success (KPIs)
In this analysis, the ideal investment opportunity (Success) was defined based on three main pillars:
1. Low Number of Auctions (Less competition/high exclusivity).
2. High Number of Assets (More options and market depth).
3. High Total Sales Volume(Strong financial returns).
4. #Tech Stack
* Python (Core language)
* Pandas (For extensive data cleaning, filtering, and aggregation)
* Jupyter Notebook / Google Colab
* #Data Source
Data was sourced from trusted, official national data portals (Open Data).
#Strategic Recommendations & Insights
Based on the data analysis, the client was presented with two distinct strategic paths depending on their investment style:

* Strategy 1: High-Monitoring / Opportunity Hunting
  * Advice: If you prefer closely monitoring the market, tracking trends, and seizing high-value opportunities, we recommend investing in [اسم المدينة الأولى هنا مثل Riyadh], specifically focusing on Q2 and Q4.

* Strategy 2: Steady Volume / Mid-Market Stability
  * Advice: If you prefer continuous buying and selling activity throughout the year with moderate, reasonable pricing, we recommend investing in [اسم المدينة الثانية هنا مثل Jeddah]across all quarters of the year.
